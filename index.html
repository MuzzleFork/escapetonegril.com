Facilities at Negril Beachfront Family Villa

Located just 37 miles from Montego Bay (MBJ) airport, a 4 bedroom, 3 bathroom, fully air-conditioned, all-inclusive private beachfront getaway — a new and exclusive offering, fully renovated, 5-star platinum experience for up to 10 guests.

Indulge and relax, allow us to fully serve your every need! This premium family-friendly beachfront villa is located at the exclusive Little Bay Country Club resort in Negril Jamaica. Other resorts simply cannot provide the same level of personalized service as our staff, all hand-picked to serve! We have 24 hour security as well as full time access (8am-8pm) to your own concierge team!

Our all-inclusive getaway includes:

  • Food and drinks (alcohol options available)
  • Full time personal chef
  • Daily cleaning service
  • Full time butler
  • Personal driver/chauffeur

Recreation

Also included is Tennis and Basketball, as well as many other water and land sports, such as paddle boarding (2 boards, each with max capacity of 350 pounds are available for our guests).

Little Bay Country Club

Just steps away from our villa, Little Bay Country Club provides full access to a private and breathtaking infinity pool that overlooks white sands and the blue ocean of Little Bay in Eastern Negril. Our cove is world-renowned and equipped with breathtaking reefs as well as amazing marine life, all located within a protected fish sanctuary.

Nearby

Outside of our private resort, we are just minutes away from the world-famous Ricks Cafe, Jimmy Buffett’s Margaritaville, Kool Runnings Water Park and of course the Jungle nightclub. In addition, all the wonderful downtown Negril amenities (and some outside of Negril) are available to you throughout your stay... day trip to Dunn’s River Falls, Parasailing across the Negril coast, spa treatments, night out on the town, etc? Our staff is here to help you plan these activities in advance and/or once you arrive.
