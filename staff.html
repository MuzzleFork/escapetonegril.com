The one and only, Eddie the Butler here to serve you and your every need.

  • Organizing local activities.

  • Making reservations for dining out, massage, excursions, etc.

Your full-time chef is here to prepare your favorite meals, including authentic Jamaican cuisine.

Your private chauffeur is available day and night.

Your Cleaning Team

No need to lift a finger on your relaxing Jamaican holiday.

  • Every room, every day.
  • Pack less - we'll even do your laundry in-house.
